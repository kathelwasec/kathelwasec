MOTTO

Soaring To Excellence.

MISSION

To effectively utilize the available resources to equip the learners with relevant skills to fit in the society.

VISSION

To provide quality education and equip the learners with skills that will enable one to fit in the society.

OUR CORE VALUES

i. Integrity-to be a community that exercises and inculcates into our learner's honesty, uprightness and purity of character.

ii. Teamwork-to encourage cooperative effort among all members of the School community.

iii. Time Management-to be good time managers and act promptly to issues considering that time is an important resource that must be utilized prudently.

iv. Efficiency-we strive to achieve the highest value of benefit from employment of resources, particularly to the learner.

v. Courtesy-we shall endeavor to become courteous to all clients at all times.

vi. Respect for individual differences-we are committed to providing education to all learners, including those with special needs.

vii. Talent development-promote individual talent for human development.

viii. Devine Godliness-revere the God is the creator of heavens and earth.
